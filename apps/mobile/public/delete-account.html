Delete your account and data

Watch Hoard · Last updated: 12 July 2026

You can delete your Watch Hoard account and its associated data at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Watch Hoard and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the two-step prompt. Your account and data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Send an email to watchhoard@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account, with the subject "Delete my account". We will process the request and confirm once your account has been removed.

Delete only some of your data (without deleting your account)

You do not have to delete your whole account to remove data. Inside the app you can, at any time: remove individual shows or movies from your library, delete your ratings, reviews and comments, delete custom lists, and unfollow other users. These changes remove that data from our systems while keeping your account active.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your personal data and content, including: your profile (email, display name, avatar), your tracked shows and movies, watch history and progress, ratings, reviews and comments, custom lists, badges, and your follow relationships.

What is kept, and for how long

We do not retain your personal data after deletion for any ongoing use. Residual copies may remain in encrypted backups and standard server logs for a limited period (up to 30 days) before they are automatically overwritten. We may retain the minimum information required to comply with legal obligations or to prevent abuse, where the law requires it.

Questions about deleting your data? Email watchhoard@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
