Watch Hoard

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

Watch Hoard ("Watch Hoard", "we", "us") is an app for tracking the TV shows and movies you watch. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By creating an account or using the app you agree to this policy.

In short: we only collect what we need to run your account and sync your watch data across devices. We do not show ads, we do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and we do not sell your data. You can delete your account and all your data at any time from inside the app.

1. Who is responsible for your data

Watch Hoard is operated independently. For any privacy question or request you can reach us at watchhoard@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide the service:

CategoryExamplesWhy
Account information Email address, password (stored only as a secure hash), display name, and an optional profile avatar you upload. To create and secure your account and let you sign in across devices.
Your content & activity Shows and movies you track, watch history and progress, ratings, reviews and comments, custom lists, badges, and who you follow. This is the core function of the app — tracking what you watch and syncing it.
Imported data If you choose to import your watch history from another service, the export file you upload, which we match to titles using public database IDs. Only processed when you actively start an import. You control whether to do this.
Technical data Standard server logs (e.g. IP address and request time) generated by our hosting and backend providers. Security, abuse prevention, and keeping the service reliable.

We do not knowingly collect precise location, contacts, health data, or any special categories of personal data.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the app: store your watch data and sync it across your devices.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To send essential account emails (for example, email confirmation and password resets). We do not send marketing email.
  • To operate social features you opt into, such as public reviews, profiles, and following other users.
  • To prevent abuse, enforce our guidelines, and comply with legal obligations.

Legal bases (GDPR): performing our contract with you (running your account), your consent (optional features such as import and social sharing), and our legitimate interest in keeping the service safe and functional.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it for advertising. Information is only shared:

  • With the service providers listed above, to operate the app.
  • With other users, but only the content you choose to make visible — for example your public profile, reviews, and follow activity. You control this through your settings.
  • Where required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service.

6. Data retention and deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account inside the app. Deleting your account removes your profile and associated watch data from our systems. You may also email watchhoard@gmail.com to request deletion. Backups and provider logs may retain residual copies for a limited period before being overwritten.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are never stored in plain text. Access to your data is protected at the database level (row-level security) so that you can only access your own records. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Children

Watch Hoard is a general-audience app and is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Most of this can be done directly in the app; for anything else, contact watchhoard@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. International transfers

Your data is primarily stored in the European Union. Some providers may process data in other countries; where that happens, appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) are relied upon.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email watchhoard@gmail.com.
